Play! Authenticate

Looking for your next social plugin?

Play! Authenticate is a brand-new choice for Java-based authentication
within Play framework applications, supporting common OAuth providers
such as Facebook in parallel with your own user account service.

Check out the showcase to see examples of Play! Authenticate in action.

Supported providers

Play! Authenticate provides authentication using
✔ Facebook ✔ Google
✔ Foursquare ✔ Proprietary*
* Using Play! Authenticate, you can even offer your very own account management

Advantages

Fully customizable

Play! Authenticate supports customization of views, allowing you to define your own look and feel for all pages. Built with simplicity and openess in mind, it is extendable in nearly every direction.

Linking and merger

Play! Authenticate has built-in support for account linking and merger, allowing your users to link new providers to their accounts and merge them.

Deadbolt support

Play! Authenticate works hand in hand with authorization module Deadbolt, pushing your app to a full-fledged web application immediately.
